翻訳サンプル

🌸 日本語

日本には数多くの天才ヴァイオリニストがいます。その中でも特に注目を集めているのは、現在13歳のHIMARIさんです。彼女は10歳までに日本や欧州の42のコンクールで1位を獲得し、10歳でアメリカのカーティス音楽院に最年少で合格しました。

🌍 English

Japan is home to many brilliant violin prodigies, but one who stands out in particular is 13-year-old HIMARI. By the age of 10, she had already won first place in 42 competitions across Japan and Europe, and at that same age, she became the youngest ever to be accepted into the Curtis Institute of Music in the United States.

🌎 Tagalog

Maraming magagaling na batang biyolinista sa Japan, ngunit ang isa sa mga pinaka-kapansin-pansin ay si HIMARI, na kasalukuyang 13 taong gulang. Bago pa siya mag-10 taong gulang, nanalo na siya ng unang gantimpala sa 42 na kumpetisyon sa Japan at Europa, at sa parehong edad ay siya ang pinakabatang tinanggap sa Curtis Institute of Music sa Estados Unidos.
